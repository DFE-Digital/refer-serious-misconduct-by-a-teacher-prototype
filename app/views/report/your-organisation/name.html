The Teaching Regulation Agency (TRA) will only consider cases of serious misconduct by teachers who are currently (or have previously been) employed or engaged at:

  • a school in England
  • a sixth form college in England
  • relevant youth accommodation in England
  • a children’s home in England
  • a 16 to 19 Academy

TRA will not usually consider cases relating to teaching assistants, higher level teaching assistants or other support staff.
